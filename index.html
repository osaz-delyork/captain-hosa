ABOUT

The Olu Of Warri is the paramount traditional ruler of the Warri Kingdom and leader Of All Itsekiri People Living In Nigeria and in the diaspora. He is the custodian of a shared heritage that traces its history back over hundreds of years. He is a figure that represents and embodies the pride and flamboyance of what is a celebrated people of the Niger Delta region.

As Olu of Warri, Omoba Tsola Emiko is a monarch that stands at the intersection of what is our highly revered traditional institutions and the dynamism of a rapidly evolving information age, essentially serving as a bridge between the old and the new. He will indeed be the peopleâ€™s monarch.
